Temporarily suspending Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021 Festival

In the face of the situation that the Covid-19 epidemic is breaking out again and has a tendency to become complicated and unpredictable; The People's Committee of Hue City, the Organizing Committee of Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021 decided to suspend the organization of Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021 in order to join with the whole province to focus all resources to control and reverse the epidemic.
