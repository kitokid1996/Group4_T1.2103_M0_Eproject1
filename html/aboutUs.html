About Asia Festival Center

The center was established with 4 members to greet you and wish you good health to participate in many festivals

Festivals have long been associated with the individual cultures of each country. Festivals are expressed from people's customs and habits and that create characteristics in different countries. Festivals are also a culture. history, to remind us.

In today's hustle and bustle life, festivals help us to look back to the past so that we can remember the merits of those who went before to create festivals for hundreds of years. The unique feature of festivals and dispelling the troubles in life and festivals is a precious way for us to remember our culture most deeply.

We look forward to receiving your support and cooperation, and hope that you will join our hands and contribute to the preservation and promotion of cultural quintessence.
