Hue City People's Committee has just issued Official Letter No. 2028 /UBND-VH on adjusting the organization time and programs of Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021.

Accordingly, in order to prepare well for the organization of Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021, the City People's Committee and the Organizing Committee of Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021 would like to inform agencies, units, departments, unions, socio-political organizations, armed forces, businesses, schools located in the city, central and local press agencies and People's Committees of wards on a number of related contents. Until the time of organizing Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021 as follows:

1. About the organization time:

Hue Traditional Craft Festival 2021 will take place from May 29 to June 26, 2021 (Opening ceremony on June 12, 2021 and Closing ceremony on June 18, 2021).

2. About the proposed program:

2.1. Official events:

- Handmade product design contest: time: April 15, 2021 to June 7, 2021.
- Sculpture camp "Revival": time of organization: May 28 - June 6, 202 at Kim Long Park.
- Hue Ao Dai Fashion Week - Living Heritage: held time: May 29 - June 5, 2021 at the pedestrian street on the Perfume River.
- Hue Book Fair 2021: organization time: May 29 - June 6, 2021 at Nguyen Dinh Chieu Walking Street and Four Statues Park.
- Wood Sculpture Exhibition "Revival": time to be held: June 7 - June 26, 2021 at Kim Long Park.
- Hue Food Festival: organization time: May 29 - June 26, 2021 at Thuong Bac Park.
- Opening of Hue Traditional Craft Festival: time: June 12, 2021 at Ky Dai.
- Space Honoring Vietnamese and International Traditional Crafts: organization time: June 12 - June 18, 2021 at Nguyen Dinh Chieu pedestrian street, Axis of cultural and art space on Le Loi street, Van center Culture, Information and Sports (base 25 Le Loi)
- Exhibition of handmade product design: time to be held: 12/6 - 18/6/2021 at Hue Cultural Center - 15 Le Loi
- Ceremony of Sacrifice of Bach Nghe Ancestor & Procession: time of organization: June 18, 2021 at Nghinh Luong Dinh.
- Closing Hue Traditional Craft Festival: time: June 18, 2021 at Phu Van Lau & Nghinh Luong Dinh.
- Talkshow I have a dream – I have a dream: time: June 15 - June 18, 2021 at the Cultural Center of Craft Village - 15 Le Loi.
- Hue Ca Festival: held time: June 20 - June 26, 2021 at many locations
- Voting for typical handicraft products: held time: 12/6 - 18/6/2021 at Nguyen Dinh Chieu pedestrian street.
- Music night in memory of Trinh Cong Son - Early mornings in Vietnam: time: June 13, 2021 at Ky Dai Square.
- Street performance festival "Hue Tuong" - A thousand years of echoes: time of organization: 07:30, 13/6/2021; route: Binh An Street - Chi Lang - Tran Hung Dao - Le Duan - Nghenh Luong Dinh - Quang Duc Gate - 23/8 Street - Hien Nhon Gate - Duyet Thi Duong.
- Beer Street: time of organization: June 18 - June 22, 2021 at Trinh Cong Son Street.
